Freiberg Online German Academy & Educational Consultancy, based in Trivandrum began its journey in virtual training of German interested enthusiasts during the post-pandemic season in 2021. Being a start-up with dedicated team onboard with passionate tutors, we have drawn students and parents’ attention through word of mouth by our successful students. Our prime motive is to equip its learners, adequate fluency in German language prior to the level they opt to enrol with us from level A1 to B2.

Why Germany?

  • Germany holds top-premier institutes in almost every field from Business Management, Science and Technology, Engineering, IT, Automobiles, Humanities providing world-class education.

  • Most of these institutes do not charge tuition fees, so students can bare the living expenses alone, which is quite affordable.

  • Courses are updated frequently to be at par with the global technological advancements.

  • Many Universities offer courses in English, which you may would be familiar with as medium of study.

  • You get to meet and exchange cross-cultural ties and develop your network with students, faculty and experts from around the world.
